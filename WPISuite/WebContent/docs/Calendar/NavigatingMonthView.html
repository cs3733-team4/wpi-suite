Navigating Month View


The Month view displays all of the weeks of a selected month with the names of each event organized by day.


To apply this view, select the Month button at the top right of the module.


To navigate between months, click the arrow buttons at the top left of the calendar to jump forward or backward by a month at a time.The Previous Arrow navigates to the previous week, while the Next Arrow navigates to the upcoming week. Alternatively, click on desired day on the Mini Calendar to bring it up that week in view. The arrow keys on the keyboard can also be used to navigate one month at a time.
