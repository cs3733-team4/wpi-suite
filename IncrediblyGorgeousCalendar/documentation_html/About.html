About


Team YOCO (You Only Compile Once) was formed for Software Engineering (CS3733) at Worcester Polytechnic Institute.  As a part of the class, teams are tasked with creating large applications as a part of a collaborative effort.  In the fall of 2013 teams were tasked with creating a calendar module for WPI Suite.
